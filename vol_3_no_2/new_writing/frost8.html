A THEORY OF THE VOWEL


by L


establish first


vowels


do not travel


well


long or short


ë é è


ö ü even i


& these

are only the Western


ones


I L


holding my


Montepulciano


a-forth

a mano!


a Milano!


ever to make


great the bastard American diphthong


shout

pout

pound
