Elisabeth Frost

Author Biography

 

Elisabeth Frost is the author of The Feminist Avant-Garde in American Poetry (2003) and the co-editor of Innovative Women Poets: An Anthology of Contemporary Poetry and Interviews (2006). She recently completed a manuscript of prose poems, All of Us, and is currently working on _L Travels_, a quasi-epistolary book-length poem. An associate professor of English at Fordham University in New York City, she directs Poets Out Loud and edits the Poets Out Loud Prize series with Fordham University Press.
