noontime ritual


keyed lock

metalbox

 

yield from surge


of glossy

catalogue

 

lodged low with bills

public name

below windowed

cellophane


a private word!

 

How can

says L

say all? to stay!

 

delaydelay


L reluctant


One last I swear!


O selfish L

to test

a way word

vow
