Bio: Rachel Loden’s book Hotel Imperium won the Contemporary Poetry Series Competition of the University of Georgia Press and made the San Francisco Chronicle’s annual top-ten poetry list. She is also the author of a prizewinning chapbook, The Last Campaign, and her poems have appeared in The Paris Review, Jacket, Overland, New American Writing, the Best American Poetry series and the Pushcart Prize anthology. In 2002 she received a California Arts Council Fellowship in Poetry. Website: http://www.thepomegranate.com/loden/
