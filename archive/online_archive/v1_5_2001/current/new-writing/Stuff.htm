Stuff

 

 

(Read at a fast pace, pausing only for breath)

 

 

 

 

(text page)

 

 

There’s no Baroque significance of the type I thought I heard, your voice seems so sad, so lost and wistful, without sounding crass is everything that bad?  With you sitting there how could I not frame you beautifully, you are my mother, there is no choice, I will always erase the whole picture and what of it, who cares.  But you have been my subject for so long.  An immeasurable blankness takes over, reducing me to inertia, a happy spectator, observing undulating speech, gestures, whilst narratives continually repeat themselves, in ever complex versions.  Is this what we know as love… then I too take part in the repeats, in the reconstructions and find myself following where you lead me.  You hate cards being laid out prophesysing doom and pain, and yet somewhere you knew that it was all about you, but the cards became the constant reminder and the throwing of the pack says it all.  I would have wanted to grab it, to alter it, to kick and shout, to buy some more or use them, to show some disrespect.  But the saints and the sinners undermined your consciousness and I don’t carry that, I’ve got the majesty and exotica of the prayer beads and the incense, but you spoke in the confession box, and stood counting your steps to heaven and hell, how could you buck it… I could sit staring at the July sky ceiling, secretly dissolving chocolate in my mouth, but your tongue was witness to the rice paper and wine.  There’s the voice of the foreigner who feels strangely dislocated from her surroundings, a world internally trapped.  And where did your world start to crumble in my eyes, or is this the usual independence lost and achieved.  Is it your voice, the special voice of the mother, who I could listen to repeatedly without getting tired or fall asleep without waking.  If I put my world in place of yours, my strange world with its stars and players, this provides nothing apart from comparisons, it does not let the imagination soar.  Here, I’ll place a soundtrack, here, describing sounds, thick, full, aural moments, a chaotic reversal of the image… but  why the additions. My enclosures are there at this moment because I need them, not for others to endure, or me to stay with.  I believe this brings up too many complications to detangle in such a short time, I find I am saying nothing at all… this leaves me to think, the pictures were just fine.  Writing can etch too hard a certain look, let a phrase gain a significance which is too easy and you’d have to face that.
