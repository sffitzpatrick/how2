Sophie Levy

Working Note

Cybernymph darting off. Reflecho. Fléche an arrowhead entering v for velocity as voice. She reflects on the rise and fall of the pretty boy; the silver of contemporary design. Lines as concentric ripples, cutting through such mirrorings.

 

 

 

The Echo Foxtrot










Bio: Sophie Levy is currently a doctoral candidate in English and Women’s Studies at the University of Toronto, working towards a thesis on models of translation and transformation in contemporary experimental women’s writing. Her work has appeared in unHERd, Kiss Machine, Masthead, The CCCP Reader and on BBC Radio Scotland. Her first book Marsh Fear/Fen Tiger (with Leo Mellor) appeared in April 2002 from Salt.
