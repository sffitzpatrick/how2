Titanic Operas

http://jefferson.village.virginia.edu/dickinson/titanic

Titanic Operas is a corner devoted to contemporary poetry seen in response (or merely in influence) to Emily Dickinson. Currently we have two “folios”; the first is a selection of essays and readings by women poets celebrating the 100 year anniversary of Dickinson’s death, and has both text and audio from such poets as Gwendolyn Brooks, Adrienne Rich, Alicia Ostriker, Sharon Olds, and Mary Oliver to name just a very few. The second folio, edited by Joe Donahue, is dedicated to contemporary poetics on the web, and has original selections from Kathleen Fraser, Maureen Owen, Fanny Howe, and Sophie Hawkes, again to name just a few.
