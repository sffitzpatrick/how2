How this page works…

InPrint is expanding as a resource for contemporary poetry & writing by women. Websites for small presses, or available contact details, are listed beside the first title to appear alphabetically from a press. If you can’t find a link, check the list above to see if it appears in an earlier reference. See also How2 Links page. Inclusions for the next issue can be e-mailed to Kate Fagan: kfag6311@mail.usyd.edu.au
